Fried Rice

Home

Description

Fried rice is a dish made by stir-frying cooked rice with various ingredients like vegetables, eggs, meat, or seafood, and seasonings. It originated in China and is popular throughout East and Southeast Asia and even in some South Asian cuisines

Ingredients

  • ⅔ cup chopped baby carrots
  • ½ cup frozen green peas
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced (optional)
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 cups leftover cooked and chilled white rice
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce, or more to taste
  • 2 tsp sesame oil, or to taste

Steps

  1. Assemble Ingredients.
  2. Place carrots in saucepan, cover with water, bring to low boil, cool 3-5 min. stir in peas, then immediately drain in colander
  3. Heat a wok over high heat. Pour in vegetable oil, then stir in carrots, peas, and garlic; cook for about 30 seconds. Add eggs; stir quickly to scramble eggs with vegetables
  4. Stir in cooked rice. Add soy sauce and toss rice to coat. Drizzle with sesame oil and toss again.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!
