Lime Tea

Home

Description

Lime tea is a beverage made by infusing tea leaves with limes, which can be served hot or cold and may be sweetened with sugar or honey.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups water
  • 1/2 tsp black tea leaves
  • Juice from one lime
  • Sugar to taste

Steps

  1. Bring water to boil in a saucepan.
  2. As the water starts boiling, add in the tea leaves and bring it to a rolling boil.
  3. Switch off as soon as the color is light brown. Add the juice of the lime and mix well.
  4. Sweeten to taste and serve immediately.
